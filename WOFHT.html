WHAT IS A SUPERCAR ?

A supercar – also called exotic car – is a loosely defined description of certain high-performance street-legal sports car. Since the 2000s or 2010s, the term hypercar has come into use for the highest performing supercars.In the United States, muscle cars were often referred to as "supercars" during the 1960s.
